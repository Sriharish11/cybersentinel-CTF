What is a CTF?

A CTF (Capture The Flag) is a fun cybersecurity competition where you solve puzzles and hacking challenges to find hidden "flags." Each flag you find earns you points!

Types of Challenges

  • Web Challenges: Find and fix website security issues.
  • Crypto Challenges: Break secret codes and ciphers.
  • Reverse Challenges: Figure out how programs work and find hidden secrets.

Who Can Play?

Anyone! Whether you're a beginner or a pro, you'll find something fun and challenging.

How to Start?

  1. Pick a challenge from the categories above.
  2. Read the instructions and hints.
  3. Solve the puzzle and submit the flag.
  4. See your score go up!
